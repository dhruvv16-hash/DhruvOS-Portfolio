## Use Case Recommendations

### Choose Option 1 if you're applying to:
- Creative agencies
- Startup tech companies
- Design-focused roles
- Companies that value bold innovation

### Choose Option 2 if you're applying to:
- Traditional tech companies
- Corporate environments
- Backend/Engineering roles
- Consulting firms
- Most general applications

### Choose Option 3 if you:
- Want maximum flexibility
- Plan to A/B test different approaches
- Want to keep original sections intact
- Have multiple videos to showcase

---

## The Verdict

**🏆 Winner: Option 2 (Video Portrait in About Section)**

**Reasoning:**
1. Strikes perfect balance between professional and engaging
2. Places video in natural "getting to know you" context
3. Doesn't compete with your name/CTA in Hero
4. Most likely to be well-received by diverse recruiters
5. Creates memorable impression without overwhelming
6. Best mobile experience
7. Gradient glow effect is eye-catching but tasteful

**Second Place: Option 3 (Dedicated Section)**
- Great if you want to test different placements
- Safest option that doesn't alter existing sections

**Third Place: Option 1 (Video Background)**
- Amazing visual impact
- Best for creative/design-forward roles
- May be too bold for traditional companies
